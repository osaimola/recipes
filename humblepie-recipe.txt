Ingredients
-1 teaspoon of misinformation
-2 cups of arrogance
-a handful of confidence
-a ton of facts
-1 link to credible proof
-several witty clapbacks

Directions
1. pour misinformation in a pan and allow to simmer till it turns golden brown and fills room with smoke.

2. add arrogance while stirring

3. put mixture in a bowl and add some confidence while still hot

4. drop ton of facts, credible proof and several witty clapbacks all in at once and turn up the oven heat.

5. serve with a smile,
